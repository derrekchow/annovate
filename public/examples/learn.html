Privacy
Governance
Framework Policy
D2L
is committed to protecting the privacy of all from whom it collects Personal
Information.
This Framework Policy sets out the general terms of
D2L
’s Privacy Governance, which
terms are applicable to the
Privacy Policies
.
Definitions
D2L means the D2L
family of companies.
External Party
means
a party external to
D2L
that has provided
D2L
with its own or its
users’ Personal Information (excluding Personal Information of users of
D2L
enterprise
offerings that are not hosted by
D2L
or its service providers, unless that Personal
Information
is collected by
D2L
in the course of
providing support services).
Personal Information
means
any
information
about an
individual
who is either identified
by the information or could be reasonably identified
.
Personal information includes
information that relates to an individual’s personal characteristics (e.g. gender, age,
income, home address, personal phone numb
er, ethnic background, family status, etc.),
health (e.g. health history, health conditions, health services received, etc.), or activities
and view (e.g. religion, politics, opinions expressed by the individual, etc.).
Personnel
means
D2L
personnel (inclu
ding contractors and consultants)
.
Privacy
and Security Governance
Committee
means
a
group of
D2L personnel
charged
with recommending and developing corporate practices and procedures for managing
privacy
-
related risks and concerns to ensure that
D2L
complies with all applicable
privacy laws.
Privacy Officer
means
a
senior leader appointed to manage
D2L
’s Priva
cy Policy and
chair the Privacy
and Security Governance
Committee.
Privacy Policies
means
, collectively,
the
D2L
Privacy Governance Framework Policy, the
D2L
Privacy Policy for External Data, the
D2L
Privacy Policy for Employee Data and any
other
policy that may be
adopted
from time to time
by the
D2L
Privacy
and Security
Governance
Committee
to
govern
D2L
’s collection, use or disclosure of Personal
Information.
